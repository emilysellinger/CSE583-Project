Map of bird sightings and PM 2.5 readings in Oregon

Use the dropdown menus and slider above to specify the family and species you are interested and the month and day you want to look at. The map will automatically update to display this information (though it may take a few moments to load). The size of the circles represents the relative number of birds seen in that location. Hover over these circles to find the exact sighting location and number of birds that were seen.

Number of bird sightings over time

Use the dropdown menu to specify the Oregon County you are interested in. Select species, family, or order to see patterns at different levels of biological organization. You can click on a marker in the legend to remove it from the graph. Double click on a species in the legend to only look at sightings of that particular species. Hover over a point to find out the name of the species, the date the observation was made and the number of birds that were seen. Days where the EPA Air Quality index was in the Very Unhealthy category are highlighted in yellow, and days in the Hazardous category are highlighted in red.
